# 🚚 Smart Supply Chain Disruption Prediction Dashboard

A comprehensive **Power BI dashboard** designed to analyze and predict supply chain disruptions using synthetic logistics data. The dashboard provides actionable insights into shipment delays, supplier performance, transportation costs, and disruption risks to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

Supply chain disruptions can significantly impact business operations by increasing delivery delays, transportation costs, and customer dissatisfaction.

This project provides an interactive Power BI dashboard that helps monitor logistics performance, identify bottlenecks, evaluate supplier reliability, and predict future disruptions using historical shipment patterns.

---

# 🎯 Objectives

- Monitor overall supply chain performance
- Analyze shipment delays across multiple locations
- Evaluate supplier performance and reliability
- Compare transportation modes
- Identify high-risk routes
- Predict future shipment disruptions
- Support data-driven logistics decisions

---

# 📊 Dashboard Pages

## 1️⃣ Supply Chain Overview

Provides a high-level summary of the entire logistics network.

### KPIs

- Total Shipments
- Average Delay Hours
- Delayed Shipments
- Transportation Cost
- Supplier Rating

### Visualizations

- Monthly Shipments
- Monthly Transportation Cost
- Average Delay by Month
- Shipment Date Filter
- Transport Mode Filter
- Country Navigation Buttons

### Key Insights

- 55K total shipments were analyzed.
- Average delivery delay is **12.11 hours**.
- More than **52K shipments experienced delays**.
- Transportation cost exceeded **494 Million**.
- Supplier performance rating remained around **4.13 / 5**.

---

## 2️⃣ Route Analytics

Analyzes logistics routes and identifies inefficient transportation paths.

### Visualizations

- Top High-Risk Routes
- Route Performance Ranking
- Delay by Route
- Transportation Cost vs Delivery Delay

### Key Insights

- Several international routes consistently experienced higher delays.
- Longer routes generally resulted in increased transportation costs.
- Certain routes showed significantly higher disruption risks than others.

---

## 3️⃣ Transportation Analytics

Compares transportation modes and their operational efficiency.

### Visualizations

- Shipments by Transport Mode
- Average Delay by Transport Mode
- Transportation Cost by Mode

### Key Insights

- Ship transport handled the highest shipment volume.
- Air transport had the highest transportation cost.
- Truck transportation maintained moderate delivery delays.
- Rail transportation showed comparatively lower delays.

---

## 4️⃣ Supplier Analytics

Evaluates supplier performance and shipment quality.

### Visualizations

- Top Suppliers by Delay
- Top Suppliers by Shipment Count
- Supplier Rating Gauge
- Supplier Performance Ranking

### Key Insights

- 20 suppliers were evaluated.
- Supplier ratings averaged **4.13 / 5**.
- A few suppliers consistently generated higher delivery delays.
- Top-performing suppliers maintained high shipment volumes with fewer disruptions.

---

## 5️⃣ Disruption Prediction Analytics

Provides predictive insights using historical shipment trends.

### Visualizations

- Future Delay Forecast
- Predicted Delay Risk Matrix
- Average Predicted Delay
- Risk Category Distribution

### Key Insights

- Average predicted delay is **12.11 hours**.
- Total disruption risk score reached **28.03**.
- High-risk shipments represent the largest disruption category.
- Historical shipment trends can be used to forecast future delivery delays.
